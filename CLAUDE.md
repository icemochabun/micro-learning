# Micro-learning Project — CLAUDE.md

## Project purpose
Developing a micro-learning curriculum for social service and senior care organisations,
based on the Comprehensive Analysis of Micro-learning Architectures (2026).

## Audience segments
- Care staff (deskless, time-constrained, high-stakes)
- Senior learners (geragogy principles apply — see /design/geragogy-ux-checklist.md)
- Volunteers (intrinsically motivated, low time commitment)

## Module format standard
- One objective per module
- Duration: 2–10 minutes
- Always ends with active retrieval (quiz or scenario)
- Templates in /modules/_templates/

## Key constraints
- Mobile-first (minimum 44px tap targets)
- Must work on WhatsApp/SMS for remote settings
- Senior-facing content: 16pt+ fonts, 4.5:1 contrast, single-tap only

## Current focus
Phase 1 pilot — care-staff clinical protocols (see /modules/care-staff/)

## Do not
- Add "nice to know" content — every sentence must earn its place
- Use abstract icons in senior-facing content
- Create modules longer than 10 minutes
