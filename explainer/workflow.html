Design principles behind the flow

🏢 Staff control stays central

  • AI generates — staff approve. Nothing reaches learners without human review.
  • Assignment is granular: staff decide exactly who sees each module and when.
  • Content can be updated at any time; learners receive the latest approved version.

🤖 What the AI actually does

  • Reads the knowledge base + system prompt to understand the objective.
  • Outputs structured flashcard pairs (term → explanation, scenario → response).
  • Generates 1–3 multiple-choice quiz questions tied to the core cards.
  • Staff can regenerate, edit individual items, or reorder before publishing.

📱 App design constraints

  • Senior-optimised: ≥18 px body text, ≥56 px tap targets, single-tap only.
  • Geragogy principles: encouragement over scores, max 2 answer choices.
  • Each lesson is 2–10 minutes — one objective, no optional detours.
  • Works on WhatsApp / SMS link-entry for lowest-tech settings.

🔁 The spaced repetition loop

  • After completion, a review is scheduled 7 days later.
  • Review replays the same Core Cards in a new random order.
  • A short re-quiz checks what was retained — score tracked over time.
  • Staff can see aggregate retention data per module per team.
